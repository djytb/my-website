djytb - hitch a ride to the end of the highway where the neons turn to wood

This project was initially presented as part of an installation. Seeking to critically engage with Sound Art, while being more drawn to music as such, elements of these respective fields are deconstructed. It tries to merge comical and serious, to evoke laughs and nostalgic scenes at the same time.
